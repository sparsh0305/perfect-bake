About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2012, Cake N Krust has been serving the people of Ghaziabad with passion and dedication for over a decade. What started as a humble neighborhood bakery has now become a local favorite, known for its authentic taste, premium quality, and heartwarming service. From a small family dream to a beloved brand, our journey has been filled with love, flour, and countless happy smiles.

At Cake N Krust, we combine traditional baking methods with modern creativity to bring you the best of both worlds. Using only the finest ingredients, each product — from our soft breads and flaky cookies to our rich pastries and elegant cakes — is crafted with care and attention to detail. Every bite reflects our commitment to freshness, flavor, and perfection.

We believe that baked goods have a special way of bringing people together. Whether it’s a birthday, an anniversary, or just a sweet craving after a long day, our creations are made to turn ordinary moments into joyful memories. Because at Cake N Krust, every celebration deserves a touch of sweetness — baked with love, and served with a smile.
